# Community Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free community. Participants should:

- Treat people with patience and professional respect.
- Critique ideas and code without attacking individuals.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and levels of experience.
- Protect private information and avoid posting real memory data, credentials, or identifying records.
- Accept maintainer decisions made to protect users, contributors, and the project.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and sustained bad-faith behavior are not acceptable.

## Scope

This code applies in project repositories, issue trackers, reviews, discussions, and other spaces where someone represents the Memery community.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository maintainers or GitHub's abuse-reporting tools. Maintainers may edit or remove content, close discussions, reject contributions, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban participants when necessary.

Enforcement decisions should consider context, severity, impact, prior behavior, and the safety of affected participants. Reports will be handled as confidentially as reasonably possible.
